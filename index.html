Utilities

Digital Nomad Token (DNT) offers several exciting utilities for token holders, including staking and governance with a high 25% annual percentage yield (APY). By staking their DNT tokens, holders can participate in the governance of the platform, such as voting on platform upgrades and funding allocation. In addition to staking and governance, DNT holders will also receive an exclusive DNT purchase card that can be used with all DNT partners in the tax-friendly countries of the Bahamas, El Salvador, Malta, Dominica, and others. This card will provide discounts and perks to users, such as discounts on hotels, restaurants, and other services. Holders of at least 1% of DNT's total supply will also receive an exclusive NFT that grants the holder access to additional travel perks in the aforementioned countries. These NFTs will be unique and will only be available to a limited number of token holders. Overall, DNT offers a unique set of utilities that aim to provide value and benefits to its holders beyond traditional use cases. With staking and governance, an exclusive purchase card, and access to additional travel perks, DNT holders will have a variety of ways to benefit from their investment in the token.

Digital Nomad Token (DNT) Whitepaper

Introduction:
The Digital Nomad Token (DNT) is a decentralized cryptocurrency built on the Ethereum blockchain. It is designed to facilitate real estate transactions in tax-friendly countries for digital nomads and other investors who value the benefits of blockchain technology. The token will also serve as a means of payment for travel perks and other goods and services in the partner countries.

TOKENOMICS:

The total supply of DNT is set at 100 million tokens, with an initial distribution of 30% allocated to the team and early investors, and 70% allocated to the public through a private sale and public sale. The token is based on the ERC-20 standard and can be stored in any Ethereum wallet that supports ERC-20 tokens. Staking and Governance DNT holders will have the ability to stake their tokens in order to earn staking rewards and participate in governance of the platform. Staking rewards will be set at a competitive rate of 25% APY to incentivize long-term holding and participation in the ecosystem. Governance will be implemented using a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) structure, where DNT holders can vote on important decisions such as platform upgrades, new partnerships, and other proposals.
Utilities:
DNT will have multiple use cases, including: DNT Purchase Card and Travel Perks DNT holders will be able to access exclusive travel perks and discounts in partner countries by using the DNT purchase card. The card will be accepted at all DNT partner merchants, including hotels, restaurants, and other businesses in the partner countries. Additionally, the purchase card will serve as a means of payment for real estate transactions on the platform. Real Estate Transactions DNT will be used as a means of payment for real estate transactions in partner countries. Digital nomads and other investors will be able to use DNT to purchase properties on the platform, which will be secured using NFTs (non-fungible tokens) to represent the ownership of the properties. This will provide a secure and efficient way to buy and sell real estate, while also leveraging the benefits of blockchain technology. Future Use Cases The Digital Nomad Token will continue to explore additional use cases for the token, including peer-to-peer lending, crowdfunding, and other DeFi features. The platform will prioritize community feedback and suggestions for new use cases to ensure that the DNT ecosystem remains dynamic and responsive to the needs of its users.
